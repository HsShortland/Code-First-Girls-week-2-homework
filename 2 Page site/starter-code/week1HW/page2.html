My Favourite Things in the Clinical Microbiology Laboratory!

  • Finding a sample with a parasitic STI called Trichomonas vaginalis
  • A bacteria that smells like caramel, called Streptococcus milleri
  • The smell of candida on yeast selective agar plates (reminds me of brewing)
  • Fluffy fungus

Trichomonas vaginalis is a parasitic sexually transmitted infection. It causes symptoms such as genital itching and burning (see the video below to see why), and can cause issues in pregnancy such as premature birth and low birth weight.
